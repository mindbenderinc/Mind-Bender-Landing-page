About

Music has the power to shape any kind of emotions, sensations that we often fail to understand when we listen to it.

Music itself has the power to dominate people's unconscious and make them vulnerable to those who can control their minds through sound.

Mind Bender was created in 2016 by João Valladão with the mission of expressing himself and providing people with unique and lasting moments through music.

Mind Bender, resident of Núcleo Biodelia, started the world of music production in 2018 focused on putting together a live show with innovative features mixing elements from different strands to form a new progressive style.

Mind Bender creates a sense of novelty and nostalgia at the same time, causing the main floor to enter an altered state of consciousness; making the moment unique and unforgettable for those who still don't know it and for those who already know it, the certainty of reliving it.

About Mind Bender

Progressive Psytrance producer, DJ and Live Act from Brazil, with releases on international labels and years of experience creating and finishing professional tracks.

Since 2018, he has been producing music and developing a unique sound that blends emotion, energy and storytelling through electronic music.

His releases include labels such as Monkey Melodic Records, 360 Music Records, Andea Records and WUTL Records.
